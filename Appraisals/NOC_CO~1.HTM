Scale of Evaluation

5Exceptional PerformanceThe employee has consistently achieved results that far exceeded the requirements in all areas, through an excellent level of diligence, commitment, dedication and innovation.
4High PerformanceThe employee has on a number of occasions accomplished more than the required level of performance and often exceeded expectations.
3Competent PerformanceThe employee has maintained the required level of performance and met expectations.
2Performance needs to improveThe employee has attempted to perform but needs to apply himself more effectively to overcome shortcomings in performance.
1Unsatisfactory PerformanceThe employee has not been effective in performing the job. Considerable improvement is required.
